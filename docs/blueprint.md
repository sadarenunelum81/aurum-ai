# **App Name**: AurumAI

## Core Features:

- Article Title Generation: Suggest suitable titles for articles based on keywords, topics, or summaries. AI is used to generate title ideas, as well as assist as a tool to suggest an alternative title to a published piece.
- Content Draft Generation: Draft a full blog post (including introduction, body paragraphs, and conclusion) from title or keywords provided, and/or from uploaded documents. It helps organize content, suggest information to include in a post, and/or check writing quality.
- Content Style Adjustment: Rewrite and adjust the style (tone, length, and complexity) of the given article according to user specifications. Tone options include formal, informal, professional, etc.
- Content Optimization: Evaluate and enhance content quality based on an SEO checklist that is incorporated as a tool within the app. Provide optimization advice, keyword density review, readability scoring, etc.
- Visual Editor: Implement a basic WYSIWYG editor interface that allows users to see how the articles will look, with formatting and styles, without requiring manual coding or previewing in separate platforms.
- Publishing Tool: Offer direct publishing to platforms such as Medium or Dev.to, streamlining the process and extending the user's reach.
- Theme Selection: Users can choose from a range of predetermined visual themes to quickly personalize the aesthetic of their content.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Rich gold (#D4AF37) to represent quality and sophistication.
- Background color: Dark charcoal (#333333) to provide a modern and contrasting backdrop.
- Accent color: Soft bronze (#CD7F32) for secondary interactive elements.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a modern serif with high contrast, suitable for headlines; body text: 'PT Sans' sans-serif font. 
- Use elegant line icons with gold highlights to maintain a consistent visual theme.
- Emphasize clear, readable content sections with ample whitespace to prevent the interface from feeling cramped or difficult to read. Gold and bronze tones may be used as rules or borders in small doses to give the composition visual structure.
- Employ subtle transitions for page loading and interactions to create a polished user experience.
